Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply book a free strategy call. We'll discuss your goals, recommend the right services, and create a custom plan for your business.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and all major credit cards. For international clients, we can also accept cryptocurrency.

Is there a contract?

Monthly retainers are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. One-time services are paid upfront before work begins.

What's your turnaround time?

It varies by service. Content creation: 3-5 days. Landing pages: 5-7 days. Ad campaigns: 3-5 days for setup. We'll always give you a clear timeline.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer unlimited revisions until you're satisfied. If we can't meet your expectations after 3 revision rounds, we'll refund 50% of the project cost.

Can I bundle services?

Absolutely! We offer discounts for bundled services. Book a call and we'll create a custom package with better pricing for you.

Is the ad budget included in the price?

No. The ad budget is paid directly to platforms like Meta or Google. Our monthly fee covers the strategy, management, creative work, and optimization of your campaigns.

Do I have to pay for AI tools separately?

Yes. You own the accounts (OpenAI, Make, etc.), so you pay the usage fees directly to them. These are usually very low (approx. $10-$30/mo) and ensure you have full control over your data.

How will I track the results?

You'll receive detailed monthly (or bi-weekly for premium plans) performance reports. We also set up a shared dashboard for real-time tracking of key metrics.
